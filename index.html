About Us

Kegees Finance is a local gold loan shop that provides quick and trusted financial support to people by offering loans against gold. We are located near Kakkanam and have been serving the local community with simple and secure gold loan services.

At Kegees Finance, our goal is to make the process of getting a loan easy for everyone. We understand that people sometimes need money urgently for things like medical needs, education, business, or personal use. That’s why we offer fast processing, low interest rates, and clear terms. There are no hidden charges, and everything is explained properly before the loan is given.

We believe in being honest with our customers. Our team makes sure that every customer feels safe and comfortable while pledging their gold. We take care of the gold by storing it safely in a high-security vault until it is returned. Every customer is important to us, and we make sure to treat them with respect and give them the best possible service.

Whether it’s your first time taking a loan or you’re already familiar with gold loans, Kegees Finance is here to help you every step of the way. With a simple process, fast service, and friendly support, we have become a trusted choice for many people in the area.
